Cycle.


An inclusive menstrual cycle tracking app.

Who is this app for?

Cycle. is an inclusive, evidence-based menstrual cycle tracking app for all menstruating people. It was designed specifically for people in queer, trans, non-binary, gender-fluid, and gender non-conforming bodies to track symptoms and dates relating to their individual menstrual cycle. This app can help you get pregnant, avoid pregnancy, or gain awareness around your cycle patterns and menstrual health. Cycle. is for people who menstruate.

How to use this app

  • Enter as much information as possible about your cycle to receive judgement-free visual feedback, important reminders, and tailored recommendations.
  • Log your symptoms regularly so you can start to track patterns and gain more awareness about your individual cycle. You can also track your BBT (basal body temperature), LH (luteinizing hormone), encounters with sperm, pregnancy test results, and many other things.
  • Know when you're most likely to be ovulating and menstruating so you can plan accordingly.
  • Check out the resources section below to learn more about hormones and the menstrual cycle.
  • Update or change your information at any time.
